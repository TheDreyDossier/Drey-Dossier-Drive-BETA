03 // OSINT KIT

Investigative literacy. These are the publicly available tools and databases Drey uses to trace corporate power. Learn to ask the questions you were never taught to ask.

Reading SEC EDGAR Filings
How to trace corporate ownership, executive compensation, and business relationships through 10-K annual reports, 8-K event disclosures, and DEF 14A proxy statements. Includes real examples from Oracle's filings.
Tracking Government Contracts
Navigate USAspending.gov, FPDS, and SAM.gov to find federal contracts by company, agency, or dollar amount. Understand NAICS codes, contract types, and classification levels.
Campaign Finance Research
Use FEC.gov to track political donations from executives and corporate PACs. Follow the money from tech companies to congressional campaigns and super PACs.
Finding Shell Companies
Search state corporate registries (Delaware, Nevada, Wyoming) to uncover subsidiary networks, registered agents, and beneficial ownership structures.
LinkedIn as Research Tool
Advanced techniques for mapping corporate networks through employee movements, job titles, and professional connections. Building organizational charts from public profiles.
Introduction to Geospatial Analysis
Using satellite imagery, property records, and IP geolocation to identify physical infrastructure. Finding data centers, corporate campuses, and government facilities.
